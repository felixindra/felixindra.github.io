Felix Indra Kurniadi

I am a Lecturer Specialist S2 at Bina Nusantara University. I am working on image processing, machine learning, deep learning in limited dataset and imbalanced dataset especially medical images or cultural images. Before working at Bina Nusantara University, I was an Informatic Engineering Lecturer at Tanri Abeng University and I also worked as an Artificial Intelligence Engineering at Delligence.ai.

I was an M.Kom student at computer science at Universitas Indonesia under the supervision Prof. Aniati M. Arymurthy . I also was a research assistant at Machine Learning and Computer Vision lab under supervision of Dr.Eng. M. Ivan Fanany

Currently I am teaching two undergraduate courses: a) Machine Learning and b) Research Method
